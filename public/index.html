SMS shopping, rides, and support

Order by text without opening ten apps.

Hakol Kaan helps customers search stores, receive item results, build carts, request rides, and get order updates through simple SMS/MMS messages.

Built for quick requests

One thread for shopping, rides, and support.

Hakol Kaan keeps the experience simple: customers can ask for store results, review prices, build carts, request ride options, confirm next steps, and get support from the same messaging flow.

Clear confirmations are shown before orders or ride requests are placed.

How it works

From first text to checkout.

The service is built around short commands, clear confirmations, and visible pricing before the customer approves an order.

Text a request

Customers can text commands like search milk, stores, cart total, ride, HELP, or STOP.

Receive results

Hakol Kaan replies with product results, store options, ride options, cart details, or account help.

Approve the next step

Customers confirm before orders or ride requests are placed, and receive updates by SMS/MMS.

Customer control

Support and opt-out stay simple.

Customers can ask for help, stop messages, and review legal details without hunting through the site.

Help stays available

Customer: HELP
Hakol Kaan: Hakol Kaan support: reply with your question or email support@hakolkaan.com. Msg frequency varies. Msg and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Opt-out stays simple

Customer: STOP
Hakol Kaan: You have been unsubscribed and will no longer receive Hakol Kaan SMS/MMS messages from this number unless you opt in again.
