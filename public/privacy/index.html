Privacy

Privacy Policy.

This policy explains what information Hakol Kaan collects and how it is used for the SMS/MMS ordering and messaging program.

  • Data we collect. We may collect your phone number, optional name, SMS/MMS message content, attachments you send, delivery address, delivery notes, order details, ride request details, prices, payment status, message credit activity, consent records, opt-in source, opt-out status, carrier delivery status, and operational logs.
  • How we use data. We use information to operate Hakol Kaan, respond to your messages, search stores, prepare carts, process orders and rides, provide support, manage message credit, send promotional or sponsored offers from Hakol Kaan, prevent abuse, and meet carrier, legal, and compliance requirements.
  • Payments. Payment card details may be handled by payment processors. Hakol Kaan does not need to publicly display or sell full card numbers to operate the messaging service.
  • SMS/MMS consent. We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers, SMS/MMS opt-in data, or text messaging consent with advertisers, affiliates, or third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties except as needed to operate the messaging service or comply with law.
  • Service providers. We may use SMS/MMS providers, payment processors, store platforms, ride providers, hosting providers, filtering providers, and support tools to operate the service.
  • Sponsored offers. Hakol Kaan may send sponsored offers from Hakol Kaan's number. Participating merchants do not receive your phone number or SMS/MMS consent records for their own text marketing.
  • Retention and security. We keep records as long as needed for operations, support, compliance, dispute handling, and legal requirements. We use reasonable safeguards such as limited access and encrypted connections where practical.
  • Your choices. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help. Email support@hakolkaan.com to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy help where applicable.
